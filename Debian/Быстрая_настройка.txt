===== Установить =====

C установочного диска Debian одной командой

''su''
''apt install aptitude mc zim xsensors curl krusader keepassxc onboard openssh-server openssh-client openvpn openconnect network-manager-openvpn vsftpd audacity calibre qbittorrent krename jlha-utils arj unace rar unrar rpm'' 
''qmmp vlc smplayer kget kdiff3 locate''
